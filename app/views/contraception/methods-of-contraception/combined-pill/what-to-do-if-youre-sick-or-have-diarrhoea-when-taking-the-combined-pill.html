What to do if you're sick or have diarrhoea when taking the combined pill

If you are sick (vomit) less than 3 hours after taking the combined pill, take another pill straight away and then take the next one at the usual time.

If you are still being sick, use another type of contraception, such as condoms, until you are able to take the pill without being sick for 7 days.

Having very severe diarrhoea may also stop the pill working properly.

If you have diarrhoea for more than 24 hours, keep taking your pill as normal. You will also need to use another type of contraception, such as condoms, while you have diarrhoea and then for 7 days after you recover.

If you are sick or have diarrhoea while taking the last 7 pills before your break (or before the dummy pills):

  • finish taking the last 7 pills
  • do not have your pill-free break – 21-day pill
  • do not take your dummy pills – everyday (ED) pill

Start your next pack straight away.


If you take the Eloine, Qlaira or Zoely pill

If you are sick (vomit) within 4 hours of taking an active pill, take your next pill as soon as possible. You need to take it:

  • not later than 12 hours after your usual time (Qlaira pill)
  • not later than 24 hours after your usual time (Zoely or Eloine pill)

If you take your next Qlaira pill more than 12 hours late, or your next Zoely or Eloine pill more than 24 hours late, read What to do if you miss a pill.
