What to do if you're sick or have diarrhoea when taking the progestogen-only pill (mini pill)

It depends on how much time has passed, and whether you take a traditional mini pill or a desogestrel mini pill.

If you're not sure, check the pack to see if your pills contain desogestrel, a type of progestogen.

The mini pill may not work if:

  • you vomit within 2 hours of taking a traditional mini pill
  • you vomit within 3 to 4 hours of taking a desogestrel mini pill
  • you have diarrhoea

Follow the advice for what to do if you miss a progestogen-only pill (mini pill).
