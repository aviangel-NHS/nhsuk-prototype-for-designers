Who can take the progestogen-only pill (mini pill)

Can I go on the progestogen-only pill?

The progestogen-only pill (or mini pill) is not suitable for everyone.

The mini pill may not be suitable if you have (or have had in the past):

  • a condition affecting your heart or blood vessels (cardiovascular disease)
  • stroke
  • a liver condition such as cirrhosis
  • breast cancer

If you have any of these health issues, talk to a GP, nurse or pharmacist about whether it's safe for you to take the mini pill.

If you cannot take contraception with oestrogen

The mini pill is usually OK to use if you cannot take the hormone oestrogen.

Oestrogen is in the combined pill, contraceptive patch and vaginal ring.
