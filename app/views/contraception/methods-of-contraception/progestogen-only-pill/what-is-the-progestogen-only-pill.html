What is the progestogen-only pill (mini pill)?

  • The progestogen-only pill (or mini pill) is a type of hormonal contraception. Progestogen is similar to progesterone, one of the body's sex hormones.
  • The mini pill stops you getting pregnant by thickening the mucus in the cervix to stop sperm reaching an egg.
  • The mini pill is more than 99% effective. When using it correctly, less than 1 in 100 women will get pregnant in a year.
  • With typical use it's 91% effective, as it's possible to miss a pill or take it late. This means 9 out of 100 women will pregnant in a year.
  • When you stop taking the mini pill, it usually takes about 1 month for your fertility to return to how it was before.
  • One kind of mini pill can also help with painful or heavy periods. This mini pill contains desogestrel, a type of progestogen.
