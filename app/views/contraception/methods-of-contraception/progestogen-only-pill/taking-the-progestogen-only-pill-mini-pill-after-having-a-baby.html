Taking the progestogen-only pill (mini pill) after having a baby

If you've just had a baby, you can start the progestogen-only pill straight away.

If you start taking it:

  • on day 1 to 21 after giving birth, you'll be protected against pregnancy immediately.
  • on day 22 or later after giving birth, use extra contraception such as condoms until you've taken the pill for 7 days.

The mini pill is also safe to use when you're breastfeeding.
