Where to get the emergency contraceptive pill (morning after pill)?

Where to get the emergency contraceptive pill

You can get free emergency contraception from:

  • most sexual health clinics, also called family planning or contraception clinics
  • most GP surgeries - you can contact your GP surgery for details
  • some NHS walk-in centres
  • pharmacies that offer NHS emergency contraception services

You can also buy the emergency contraceptive pill at most pharmacies. Many pharmacies also sell the emergency contraception pill online.

Find emergency contraception services

Getting contraception if you're under 16

Contraception is free and confidential, including for young people under the age of 16.

The doctor or nurse will not tell anyone, including your parents or carer, unless they think you or someone else is at risk of harm.

Find out more about contraception and sexual health services for young people on the Brook website.
