Side effects of the emergency contraceptive pill (morning after pill)

The emergency contraceptive pill is very safe and serious side effects are rare.

Side effects of the emergency contraceptive pill

Commonly reported side effects include:

  • headache
  • mood swings
  • tummy and pelvic pain and breast tenderness
  • tiredness
  • irregular bleeding until you have your period (and your next period may be more painful than usual)
  • feeling and being sick

If you are sick (vomit) within 2 hours after taking Levonelle or 3 hours after taking ellaOne, you will need to take another dose as soon as possible.
