  2. Review titled Wonderful care and attention

    ★★★★★

    Rated 5 stars out of 5

    by Daniel Christopher moloney - Posted on 20 December 2022

    I had surgery on my knee and the whole experience was stress free and as comfortable as major surgery can be. I was admitted with care, treated professionally and with great care and attention throughout. All key personnel saw me prior to and post my operation. The food for all meals was excellent. The nurses were supportive and friendly as was the physiotherapist. My experience at St Anthony's from beginning to end was better than I could have hoped for and I would unreservedly recommend the hospital to possible future patients.
