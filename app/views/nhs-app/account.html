Access your NHS account

Log in to:

  • book your COVID-19 vaccine
  • view your health record and test results
  • order repeat prescriptions
  • book and manage appointments and referrals
  • access other NHS services
Information:

We may need to send you a security code on your mobile phone, so you can log in.

Continue with NHS login

Who can have an NHS account

You can have an account if you are:

  • registered with a GP surgery in England
  • aged 13 or over

Find out more about who can have an NHS account

Get the NHS App

You can also log in to your NHS account using the NHS App on your smartphone or tablet.
